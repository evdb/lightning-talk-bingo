Lightning Talk Bingo

At start of lightning talks cards are handed out

Host explains what will happen

  everyone needs a card
  listen to the talks and cross out events as they happen
  if you fill a row, column or diagonal shout out bingo
  speaker gets to finish their talk
  after the talk winner says the things they got
  audience votes whether win is valid
  prize is awarded
  talks continue

If no winner emerges during talks then a list of shuffled events is read out until someone wins.

In the case of a draw the random events are used as a tie breaker.
